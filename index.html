Dr. Muhammad Yunus speaks of Bangladesh as a powerful example of resilience, innovation, and human potential. He believes Bangladesh is not defined by its challenges, but by its determination to overcome them. From its independence to its steady economic and social progress, the country has consistently demonstrated courage and unity. According to Dr. Yunus, the true strength of Bangladesh lies in its people—especially the hardworking rural communities and women entrepreneurs who are transforming their lives through opportunity. Through initiatives like Grameen Bank, Bangladesh has shown the world that poverty is not created by the poor, but by systems that limit access to opportunity. By providing small loans and encouraging entrepreneurship, millions have improved their living standards with dignity.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus
